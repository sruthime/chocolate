About Us

ChocoLuv take great pride in being one of India's select manufacturers of the finest chocolates, cocoa products and cocoa derivatives. Our products are supplied to chocolate makers and chocolate users across the world, from local bakeries to multi - national companies. Incorporated in the year 1989 and having commenced operations in 1992, Chocoluv is a well known reliable business partner for the supply of cocoa and chocolate products. Starting from the sourcing of fine quality cocoa beans to processing of cocoa beans and delivering the finest chocolates... Lotus's has a fully integrated manufacturing facility and is built with the best technologies and expertise from across the globe.
